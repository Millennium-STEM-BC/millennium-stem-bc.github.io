What is Unlocking STEM?

Millennium STEM BC presents our Unlocking STEM Speaker Series! Unlocking STEM is Millennium’s new initiative that aims to shine light on the interdisciplinary nature of STEM. Through open conversations with students and industry professionals, we hope to highlight each individual’s personal journey into their respective fields. This Speaker Series aims to explore STEM from a personal approach, providing students with a holistic sense of what a future career in STEM might hold and what they can do to reach their academic, personal, and career goals.
