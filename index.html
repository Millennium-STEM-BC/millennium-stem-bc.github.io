Millennium STEM BC

Our Mission

As a STEM youth non-profit, our organization aims to provide equal opportunities for youth in BC to pursue professions in STEM. We recognize that STEM is an extremely competitive field that many ambitious students wish to pursue; through the initiatives of our organization, we hope to create opportunities for individuals, particularly those from disadvantaged backgrounds, to develop skill sets and networks that will help them pursue their passions.
